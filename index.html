About Us

We're Kate and Katie, two Midwest girls who really, really love to snack. We grew up around potlucks, tailgates, and kitchen counters full of dips, where food wasn't fancy, but it always brought people together. Somewhere along the way, cottage cheese got left out of the mix. So we fixed that.

We whipped it, literally. We took the classic and made it smooth, flavorful, and packed with protein. No curds, no clumps: just something you actually want to dip into again and again.

Our goal? To put whipped cottage cheese back where it belongs: at the center of every get-together, from game days to late-night hangs. Because the best snacks don't just sit on the table, they bring people back for more.
