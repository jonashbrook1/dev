We Are Disco Cow

We make whipped cottage cheese dips, spreads, and sauces. We take classic cottage cheese with curds, blend it smooth and creamy, then add our favorite spices and mix-ins to create high-protein options perfect for any hosting, cooking, or snacking moment.

Cottage cheese shouldn't just sit quietly in the fridge. It should be at the center of every table, party, and snack board. When cottage cheese ruled the 70s, the dance floor was full. "Disco" is our nod to that era. The future is Disco Cow.

The Comeback Track

From the 70s peak to today's protein revolution

1970 - The Peak

  • Cottage cheese outsold yogurt. The average American ate 4.6 lbs per year. It was seen as a staple of healthy eating.

1980-2010 - The Decline

  • Consumption steadily dropped. Stagnant innovation with limited flavors and same texture. Lost cultural relevance.
